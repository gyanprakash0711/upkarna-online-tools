What is Public Provident Fund (PPF)?

PPF is a long-term savings scheme offered by the Government of India through post offices and banks. It offers guaranteed returns and tax benefits under Section 80C.

Benefits of PPF

  • Guaranteed returns
  • Tax exemption on deposit and interest
  • Flexible annual deposits
  • Safe government-backed scheme

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your annual deposit, expected interest rate, and tenure. The calculator will show maturity amount, total interest, and year-wise growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a PPF Maturity Calculator?

A PPF Maturity Calculator helps you calculate the maturity amount and interest earned on a Public Provident Fund account based on annual deposit, rate, and tenure.

How is PPF interest calculated?

PPF interest is calculated annually on the balance at the end of each year. Deposits are made annually, and interest is compounded yearly.

What is the minimum and maximum tenure for PPF?

PPF has a minimum tenure of 15 years and can be extended in blocks of 5 years up to 50 years.

Are PPF returns guaranteed?

Yes, PPF returns are guaranteed by the Government of India. The interest rate is notified annually.

How accurate is this calculator?

The calculator uses standard PPF calculation formulas and provides accurate estimates based on the entered parameters.
