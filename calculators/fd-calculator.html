What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a savings scheme offered by banks where you deposit a lump sum for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate. It's a safe investment option with guaranteed returns.

Benefits of FD

  • Guaranteed returns
  • Flexible tenure options
  • Higher interest rates than savings accounts
  • Low risk investment

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your deposit amount, expected interest rate, tenure, and compounding frequency. The calculator will show maturity amount, total interest, and year-wise growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an FD Calculator?

An FD Calculator helps you calculate the maturity amount and interest earned on a Fixed Deposit based on principal, rate, tenure, and compounding frequency.

How is FD interest calculated?

FD interest is calculated using compound interest formula: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt), where A is maturity amount, P is principal, r is rate, n is compounding frequency, t is tenure.

What compounding frequencies are supported?

The calculator supports annual, quarterly, monthly, and daily compounding frequencies.

Are FD returns guaranteed?

FD returns are guaranteed by banks up to Rs. 5 lakh under DICGC insurance. Always check current rates and terms.

How accurate is this calculator?

The calculator uses standard compound interest formulas and provides accurate estimates. Actual returns may vary based on bank policies.

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on compound interest formulas. Actual FD rates and terms may vary by bank.
