About FIRE (Financial Independence Retire Early)

FIRE is a movement focused on achieving financial independence and retiring early. The core principle is to save aggressively, invest wisely, and live below your means to accumulate enough wealth to cover your expenses through passive income.

FIRE Principles

  • High Savings Rate: Save 50-70% of income
  • Investment: Grow savings through compounding
  • Frugal Living: Minimize expenses while building wealth
  • Passive Income: Generate income without active work

Common FIRE Strategies

  • Lean FIRE: Minimalist lifestyle, lower expense target
  • Fat FIRE: Comfortable lifestyle, higher expense target
  • Barista FIRE: Part-time work with passive income
  • Coast FIRE: Stop saving but let investments grow

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 4% rule in FIRE?

The 4% rule suggests you can withdraw 4% of your portfolio annually, adjusted for inflation, with a high probability of your money lasting 30+ years.

The savings rate depends on your timeline. For FIRE in 10 years, you might need 60-70% savings rate. For 20 years, 40-50% might suffice.

Is FIRE realistic in India?

Yes, FIRE is achievable in India with disciplined saving, smart investing, and lifestyle optimization. Many Indians have successfully achieved FIRE.
