What is Net Worth?

Net worth is a measure of your financial health. It's calculated by subtracting your total liabilities from your total assets.

Why Track Net Worth?

  • Monitor financial progress
  • Make informed financial decisions
  • Set financial goals

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I calculate net worth?

Calculate it quarterly or annually to track changes.

What if my net worth is negative?

A negative net worth means liabilities exceed assets. Focus on debt reduction.
