What is CAGR?

CAGR, or Compound Annual Growth Rate, is the rate of return that would be required for an investment to grow from its beginning balance to its ending balance, assuming profits were reinvested at the end of each year.

Who Should Use CAGR Calculator?

  • Investors evaluating portfolio performance
  • Businesses analyzing revenue growth
  • Anyone comparing investment returns over time

Frequently Asked Questions

What does CAGR tell you?

CAGR provides a smoothed annual return rate, useful for comparing different investments.

Is CAGR the same as annual return?

No, CAGR is the geometric progression ratio that provides a constant rate of return over the time period.
