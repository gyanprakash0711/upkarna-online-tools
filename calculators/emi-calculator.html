What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay every month towards loan repayment.

Who Should Use This EMI Calculator?

  • Home loan borrowers
  • Car loan applicants
  • Personal loan users

Frequently Asked Questions

Does EMI remain constant?

EMI remains constant for fixed-rate loans and may change for floating-rate loans.

Can I reduce EMI?

Yes, by increasing tenure or making prepayments.
